What is it?

Basketball is a worldwide sport that involves usually five player teams with the goal of beating another team of players.
Players can score points by getting the basketball (Fig 1) to go into the hoop (Fig 2).

The Beginnings

The beginnings of basketball date back to a small gymnasium in Massachusetts. It started with nothing more than a fruit basket and a ball.

Eras

For most avid fans of the sport, it can be divided into different eras of basketball:

  • Pre ABA-NBA Merger
  • Magic & Larry Era
  • Michael Jordan Era
  • Modern Era

Michael Jordan

A legendary NBA player that is considered by many to be the greatest of all time. He pushed the NBA into a worldwide sport with his popularity.

Final Words

Basketball has grown over the years into a bigger and bigger sport and continues to grow. Each era of basketball has it's own importance to the history and has allowed the sport to grow in unique ways.
